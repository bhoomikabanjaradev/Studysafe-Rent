# StudySafe Rent

AI-powered student rental platform for safe, verified, and smart room booking. Built for students moving to new cities for studies, coaching, internships, or jobs.

## Problem Statement

Students often shift to new cities and rent rooms without proper information or safety checks.

Common challenges:

- Fake room listings
- Unsafe owners or localities
- Unknown roommates
- Parents unaware of living location
- Poor room conditions after shifting
- Difficulty finding study-friendly areas
- Lack of trust in rental platforms

## Solution

StudySafe Rent helps students find trusted and verified rentals with AI-powered recommendations, safe area insights, roommate transparency, and parent access tools.

## Key Features

### Verified Owner Listings

- Owners upload identity & property proof
- Admin verification before listings go live
- Verified trust badge

### Room Types

- Single Room
- Double Sharing
- Four Sharing
- PG / Hostel
- Full Room

### Resident Visibility (LinkedIn Style)

- View current residents as round profile avatars
- See basic compatibility preview before booking
- Full resident profile unlocks only after payment / confirmed move-in

### Parent Safety Dashboard

After booking:

- Owner details
- Property address
- Contact number
- Roommate information
- Emergency contacts

### AI Smart Recommendations

Find best rooms based on:

- Budget
- Distance from college/coaching
- Quiet study environment
- Safe locality
- Food availability
- Compatible roommates

### Smart Area Tags

For cities like Kota, Delhi, Noida, Pune etc:

- Study Zone
- Mess Zone
- Family Zone
- Student Hub
- Safe Zone
- Budget Zone
- High Demand Zone

### Ratings & Reviews

- Safety score
- Cleanliness
- WiFi
- Owner behavior
- Verified student reviews
